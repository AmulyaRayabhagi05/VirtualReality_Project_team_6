# Virtual Museum: The Timeline Muesum

An immersive VR journey through human history, built specifically for **Google Cardboard** and **Android**. Navigate through three distinct levels of time, from the dawn of civilization to the distant future.

##  Hardware & Setup
* **Headset:** Google Cardboard (or any mobile VR viewer).
* **Device:** Android Smartphone.
* **Controller:** Bluetooth Joystick Controller (for movement and interaction).

---

## The Museum Layout
The museum is designed as a vertical journey. Users start at the bottom and walk through time:

###  Bottom Floor: Ancient Times
This level features artifacts from age of the dinosaurs, ancient civilizations, and the foundation of architecture.

### Middle Floor: Modern Times
A gallery dedicated to the industrial revolution, the digital age, and contemporary art and technology.

### Top Floor: The Future
This level explores human evolution, interstellar travel, and futuristic cityscapes.

---

##  Advanced Requirements
This project implements two advanced technical features:

1. **Conversational Social Avatars**: You are able to communicate with social avatar essential a guide while you walk through the museum.
2. **Multiplayer & Voice Chat**: You are able to explore the exhibits with others in real-time and discuss the history of the world using integrated voice communication.
